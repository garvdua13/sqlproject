Netflix's library is dominated by Movies, which make up nearly 70% of all content. With 6,131 movies compared to 2,676 TV Shows, it's clear that Netflix's strategy leans heavily towards film content. However, TV Shows still represent a significant portion — nearly 1 in 3 titles — reflecting Netflix's growing investment in original and licensed series to retain subscribers long-term.
